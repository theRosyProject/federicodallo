**Horizon Europe** | GA 101157458 | Nov 2024 – Oct 2028 | €5.6M

healthRiskADAPT develops health risk assessment services and innovative adaptation options against climate-related threats — heatwaves, air pollution, wildfire emissions, and pollen — in Mediterranean, Alpine, and Continental regions. The project co-creates integrated adaptation solutions (technical, nature-based, and social) and builds interactive toolkits for local and regional authorities.

Federico Dallo is Project Partner Leader, contributing to sensor-based environmental monitoring and data analysis for health risk quantification.

**Coordinator:** NILU (Norway) | **Partners:** organisations across Europe
