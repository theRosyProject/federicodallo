**Horizon 2020** | MSCA-IF GA 844526 | Nov 2020 – Mar 2024 | €251K

PIONEER established a low-cost wireless sensor network to study transboundary atmospheric transport phenomena, with a focus on ozone monitoring. The network crossed the Alps along the 12th east meridian (Munich–Venice trail), providing high spatial resolution data for climate modelling in remote areas where conventional monitoring infrastructure is absent.

Federico Dallo was the Principal Investigator (Marie Skłodowska-Curie Fellow), based at the National Research Council of Italy (CNR) with a secondment at the University of California, Berkeley.
