**Founder** | 2021 – present

Particular Matter is an open initiative championing the right to breathe clean air. It combines low-cost sensor networks and artificial intelligence to monitor and improve indoor environmental quality across residential, institutional, and research settings.

The project grew out of the [PIONEER](https://cordis.europa.eu/project/id/844526) Marie Skłodowska-Curie Fellowship (GA 844526) and currently contributes to the Horizon Europe [HealthRiskAdapt](https://cordis.europa.eu/project/id/101157458) project (GA 101157458).
