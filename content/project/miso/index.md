**Horizon Europe** | GA 101086541 | Jan 2023 – Jun 2026 | €2.6M

MISO develops an autonomous, multi-format in-situ observation platform to accurately detect and quantify CO₂ and methane concentrations in remote permafrost and wetland areas. The platform combines stationary towers, automated gas chambers, and drone-mounted sensors, powered by geothermal devices and connected via cloud-based IoT technology.

Federico Dallo is Project Partner Leader, contributing expertise in wireless sensor network design and deployment for atmospheric monitoring in remote environments.

**Coordinator:** NILU (Norway) | **Partners:** six organisations across Europe
